About Us

Welcome to Naketo Family Catering

For over 15 years, Nakaketo Family has been bringing the taste of home to celebrations across Kampala. We specialize in family-style meals and professional outside catering for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, and community gatherings.

Every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients, traditional recipes, and the care you’d expect from family. From intimate lunches to 500+ guest events, we handle the food, setup, and service so you can focus on what matters most.
